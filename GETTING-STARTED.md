# Getting Started — Evolv AI EVOLV-OS

> Your AI Operating System. No terminal. No coding. Just Claude.

---

## Step 1: Open this folder in Claude Cowork

1. Open the **Claude desktop app**
2. Click **Cowork** (top of the sidebar, next to Chat and Code)
3. Click **New task** → select **Working folder** → choose the folder you cloned in Step 1
4. Claude now has access to everything in this folder

---

## Step 2: Say hello

Type this in Cowork:

> **"Initialize my session"**

Claude will read this workspace and introduce itself. It will tell you what it knows about your business and confirm it's ready to help.

If this is your first time, Claude will explain that your context files are empty and guide you to install ContextOS first.

---

## Step 3: Install your first module

> **"Install ContextOS"**

This is the foundation of your EVOLV-OS. Claude will interview you about your business — what you do, who your clients are, your team, your goals. It takes 30-45 minutes and is done entirely through conversation.

After ContextOS, Claude knows your business. Every session from that point on, it picks up exactly where you left off.

---

## Full install order

| # | Say this | What it does | Time |

You don't have to do them all at once. Each module is independently valuable. Start with ContextOS, then come back when you're ready.

## Daily use

Once your EVOLV-OS is set up, here's your daily rhythm:

**Start of day:**
> "Initialize my session" → Claude gives you a briefing based on your context, data, and recent signals

**During the day:**
> "Brainstorm [idea]" → Claude explores options and trade-offs with you before you decide

**Need a report or summary:**
> "Write a report on [topic]" → Claude uses your context to produce a clean, professional output

---

## What you can ask Claude

No commands to memorize. Just describe what you want:

- *"What's on my plate this week?"*
- *"Find that meeting I had with [name] last week"*
- *"Has anyone mentioned [topic] in Slack?"*
- *"I want to automate [task] — help me think through it"*
- *"Update my strategy — we just decided to focus on [X]"*
- *"Write a status update for my team"*
- *"Audit my tasks and tell me what I should automate"*
