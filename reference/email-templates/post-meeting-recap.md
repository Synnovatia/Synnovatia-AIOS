Separate from `client-reengagement/`'s post-call follow-up, which is for former/lapsed-client re-engagement calls specifically (manually triggered, not this automated flow) — no overlap between the two.

**Notes for drafting:**
- Pull the recap sentence from Zoom's "quick recap" or synthesize briefly from the full summary — don't just paste raw AI-summary text verbatim if it reads awkwardly; light editing for tone (peer not guru, warm not soft) is expected.
- Action items come from Zoom's "next steps" field. If there are none captured, omit the bullet list and just send the recap sentence plus a warm close.
- If the summary isn't ready yet (still processing), don't draft anything — wait for the next check.
