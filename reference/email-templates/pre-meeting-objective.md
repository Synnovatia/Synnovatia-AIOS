# Pre-Meeting Objective Ask

> Sent to existing/ongoing clients 4 days before a Boomerang-booked meeting, only when they didn't already give an objective at booking time. Draft-only — Jackie reviews and sends. New clients are excluded (their first meeting is handled by the onboarding welcome flow's own redirect step).

---

Subject: Ahead of our conversation on [Meeting Day/Date]

Hi [First Name],

Looking forward to our conversation on [Meeting Day, Month Date] at [Meeting Time] PT. So I can make the most of our time together — what would be most helpful to focus on this time? Anything specific on your mind, a decision you're working through, or something that's come up since we last talked.

No need for anything formal — just a line or two so I can come prepared.

Talk soon,
