## Step 1 — Copy this workspace to your computer

Your Evolv AI consultant will share a link to this repository. Once you have it:

**1. Create your own copy on GitHub**

On the repository page, click **"Use this template"** → **"Create a new repository"**

- Name it something like `my-EVOLV-OS` or your business name
- Set visibility to **Private**
- Click **"Create repository"**

> This gives you your own private copy — completely separate from the template. Your data stays yours.

**2. Download it to your computer**

Open **GitHub Desktop** → click **"File"** → **"Clone repository"**

- Find your new repository in the list
- Choose where to save it on your computer (e.g., Desktop or Documents)
- Click **"Clone"**

You now have a folder on your computer with everything inside.

---

## Step 2 — Connect it to Claude Cowork

1. Open the **Claude desktop app**
2. Click **"Cowork"** at the top of the sidebar
3. Start a new task → click **"Working folder"** → select the folder you just cloned
4. Say: **"Initialize my session"**
5. Say: **"Install ContextOS"** — this is always first

**See `GETTING-STARTED.md` for the full setup guide with screenshots and tips.**

Claude guides you through everything in plain English.
