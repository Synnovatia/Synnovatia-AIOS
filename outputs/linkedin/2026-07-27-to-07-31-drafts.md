## Monday, July 27 — Thought Leadership

In 25+ years of cross-industry consulting, I've watched growth stall in almost the same places every time — around $250K-$500K, again near $750K-$1M, again at $3-4M. The industries change, but the walls don't move.

Each of those thresholds asks you to run the business differently than the version of you that built it. And most owners reach for more hours, because more hours is what got them there in the first place.

Where did your business hit its wall — and did you change the structure, or just push harder?

## Wednesday, July 29 — Story (Messy Middle mastermind experience)

A member of the Mastermind for the Messy Middle told me what surprised her most. She expected the strategy to be the draw — and it helps — but what she keeps coming back for is a room of women running businesses her size who understand exactly what she means without needing the backstory first.

We talk growth, of course. But we also get into the client call you've been dreading, the hire you're circling, and yes, sometimes hormones and what's for dinner. Somewhere along the way, colleagues became friends.

The next cohort starts October 9. Applications are open now.

## Friday, July 31 — Client Win

A client raised her rates 30%, certain she'd lose half her client list. She ended up losing exactly one — the rest had apparently been waiting for her to charge what the work was actually worth.

I've watched dozens of owners agonize over this same decision, and the pattern holds: what feels like humility on your side of the invoice usually looks like uncertainty from theirs.
