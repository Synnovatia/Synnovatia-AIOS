You didn’t do anything wrong. You just outgrew what got you here.

Growth tends to stall in predictable places: around $250K–$500K, again near $750K–$1M, and again at $3–4M. Resource constraints pile up, and a strategy that once worked suddenly feels too small for the business you’ve built. It’s what happens between Stage I and Stage III, and almost nobody warns you it’s coming. It isn’t a personal failing.

Synnovatia works from the pattern underneath the symptom: 25+ years of cross-industry recognition, applied specifically to your business rather than borrowed from someone else’s framework.

Different is better than better.

Most consultants compete to be better along the same three or four dimensions: frameworks, credentials, hourly rates. Synnovatia sits outside that competition entirely. The difference is the combination of Fortune 500‑grade strategic expertise and genuine peer‑level relational depth, from someone running her own business alongside yours.

Ask a client what that looks like in practice, and “the 30,000‑foot view” is the phrase that comes up. It’s the vantage point that’s hard to find from inside your own business, paired with precise language for what you already sense but can’t quite name.
