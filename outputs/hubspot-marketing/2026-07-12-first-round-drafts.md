## 3. "What I'm Watching" — First Edition

**Subject:** What I'm watching for small business owners this month

Hi [First Name],

A few things I'm keeping an eye on right now, for anyone running a service business in the $350K-$4M range:

**Borrowing costs are still shaping hiring decisions.** If you've been holding off on bringing someone on because financing feels uncertain, you're not alone — a lot of the owners I talk to are making the same calculation. Worth revisiting quarterly rather than deciding once and shelving it.

**The labor market keeps tightening for specialized roles.** Finding the right person is taking longer than it used to for a lot of businesses in this range. If your hiring timeline assumptions are from a few years ago, they're probably out of date.

**Clients are scrutinizing budgets more closely, even when their own businesses are healthy.** This isn't necessarily a sign your offer is off — it's a broader climate shift. Worth having a clear, specific answer ready for "why this, why now" conversations.

None of this is a reason to freeze. It's a reason to stay informed and adjust deliberately, rather than reactively. That's the whole point of having someone outside your business who's watching the same things you are.

Jackie
