Three for three. **The `annualrevenue` field in HubSpot should be treated as unusable for screening**, not merely spotty. Every shortlist here has to be screened on Jackie's own knowledge; a low figure on file is not grounds to rule anyone out, and a figure that lands in-band proves nothing.

That suggests the roster is a stronger channel for the **Seven Figure Forum** than for the Messy Middle — the opposite of what this exercise set out to do.
