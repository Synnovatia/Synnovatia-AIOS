## Two things worth knowing

**Don't trust HubSpot's revenue figures.** Two current members contradict them outright:
- **Amy Hage** (Messy Middle) has **$50,000** on file — far below the group's advertised $250K–$500K band.
- **Mark Chapman** (Seven Figure Forum) has **$200,000** on file — a fifth of the $1M+ the Forum is named for.

And the one figure that looked trustworthy wasn't either: **Diane Leonard's $300,000** read as a perfect Messy Middle match, but Jackie places her at Seven Figure Forum level — so that record is badly out of date too.

Three for three. **The `annualrevenue` field in HubSpot should be treated as unusable for screening**, not merely spotty. Every shortlist here has to be screened on Jackie's own knowledge; a low figure on file is not grounds to rule anyone out, and a figure that lands in-band proves nothing.

**A pattern worth noticing:** every name Jackie has promoted so far moved *up*, not down. The roster is 15+ years of relationships, and those businesses have grown since the records were written. That suggests the roster is a stronger channel for the **Seven Figure Forum** than for the Messy Middle — the opposite of what this exercise set out to do.
